# Service Offering: CRM Solutions

## Overview
Description, technology stack, target customer profile, and benefits of Inurum Technologies' CRM Solutions service offering.

## Detailed Information
### Service Description
Integrating client apps with HubSpot or Salesforce and automating pipeline synchronization.

### Technology Stack
* **Core Technologies:** Node.js, REST Webhooks, CRM API SDKs, OAuth2
* **Development Standards:** Following strict ISO 9001 and secure software guidelines.

### Benefits to Clients
* Rapid delivery speed with dedicated, experienced engineering teams.
* Zero integration friction thanks to full-stack, no-handoffs coordination.
* High system reliability (99.9% crash-free session target).

### Target Customer Segments
* **Target Customers:** Sales, marketing, and client coordination teams
