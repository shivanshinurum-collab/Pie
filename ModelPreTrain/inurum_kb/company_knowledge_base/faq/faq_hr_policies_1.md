# Frequently Asked Questions: Human Resources & Company Policies (Part 1)

## Overview
Curated Q&A list covering Human Resources & Company Policies at Inurum Technologies - Part 1.

## Detailed Information
### Q01: What are the core working hours at Inurum?
* **Answer:** Core office hours are 09:00 to 18:00 (Monday to Friday) local time.

### Q02: How do I log my daily attendance?
* **Answer:** Log attendance via the internal ERP app or portal before 09:30 AM IST.

### Q03: What is the process to apply for a sick leave?
* **Answer:** Submit a request in the ERP under Leave Management and notify your manager via Slack.

### Q04: Are there paid annual leaves at Inurum?
* **Answer:** Yes, employees receive 18 paid annual leaves, accrued monthly.

### Q05: What is the work from home policy?
* **Answer:** Employees can request WFH for up to 2 days per week, subject to project manager approval.

### Q06: Does the company reimburse WFH internet bills?
* **Answer:** Yes, internet bills up to $30 (INR 2500) per month are reimbursable for regular WFH roles.

### Q07: How do I submit travel expense reimbursement claims?
* **Answer:** Submit claims with receipts in the reimbursement module by the 25th of each month.

### Q08: Who should I contact for salary slip queries?
* **Answer:** Contact Rohit Verma in Finance or write to accounts@inurum.com.

### Q09: What is the standard notice period for exit?
* **Answer:** The notice period is 60 days for permanent employees and 30 days during probation.

### Q10: Do we have health insurance coverage?
* **Answer:** Yes, group medical cover is provided for employees and their immediate dependents.

### Q11: Are employee referrals rewarded?
* **Answer:** Yes, employee referrals for technical roles receive a bonus upon successful onboarding.

### Q12: How do I report a policy violation?
* **Answer:** Report violations confidentially to Priya Mehta at hr@inurum.com.

### Q13: What holidays are observed in the India office?
* **Answer:** Inurum observes 10 gazetted public holidays in India, announced in January.

### Q14: Does the Italy office follow the same holidays?
* **Answer:** The Italy office follows the local Italian public holiday calendar.

### Q15: What is the dress code in the offices?
* **Answer:** The dress code is smart casual from Monday to Thursday, and casual on Friday.

### Q16: How is the annual performance review conducted?
* **Answer:** Performance reviews occur in April, based on KPI goals tracked in Jira.

### Q17: Can I carry forward unused annual leaves?
* **Answer:** Up to 5 unused annual leaves can be carried forward to the next year.

### Q18: What is the policy for parental leave?
* **Answer:** Maternity leave is 26 weeks; paternity leave is 2 weeks of paid time off.

### Q19: Is there study leave for certifications?
* **Answer:** Yes, employees can request up to 3 days of paid leave to take certified exams.

### Q20: Does the company cover certification costs?
* **Answer:** Yes, pre-approved certification fees are fully reimbursed upon passing.

### Q21: How are work hours tracked for dedicated customer teams?
* **Answer:** Dedicated teams log hours directly into the client's tracking system and duplicate in our ERP.

### Q22: What is the policy on intellectual property created by employees?
* **Answer:** All IP created during employment is the sole property of Inurum Technologies.

### Q23: Are team lunches reimbursable?
* **Answer:** Team lunches are reimbursable up to $15 per person with prior manager approval.

### Q24: What happens during a probation review?
* **Answer:** A review is held at the 3-month mark to confirm performance expectations are met.

### Q25: Can probation be extended?
* **Answer:** Probation can be extended up to another 3 months if performance targets are not met.
