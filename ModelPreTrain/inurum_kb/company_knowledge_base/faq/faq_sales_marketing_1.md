# Frequently Asked Questions: Sales Process & Client Onboarding (Part 1)

## Overview
Curated Q&A list covering Sales Process & Client Onboarding at Inurum Technologies - Part 1.

## Detailed Information
### Q01: What is the initial response SLA for sales inquiries?
* **Answer:** Inbound sales inquiries must be answered within 24 hours.

### Q02: How do I request a free technical consultation?
* **Answer:** Clients can schedule a 30-minute call via inurum.com/start.

### Q03: Who leads business development campaigns?
* **Answer:** Shubham Saratha is the BDE lead coordinating sales strategies.

### Q04: What is the conversion target for sales leads?
* **Answer:** Our sales goal is a conversion rate greater than 25% on qualified leads.

### Q05: Where are new leads logged?
* **Answer:** All customer leads and communications are logged in the HubSpot CRM.

### Q06: What is the standard duration of an MVP project?
* **Answer:** A standard mobile MVP ranges from 8 to 14 weeks from project kickoff.

### Q07: How is a project budget estimated?
* **Answer:** Estimates are prepared after a tech plan blueprint phase based on sprint rates.

### Q08: What is the turnaround time for a custom quote?
* **Answer:** Custom quotes and feasibility reviews are delivered in 12–24 hours.

### Q09: How do we market our technical capability?
* **Answer:** Through developer blog posts, case studies, and LinkedIn updates.

### Q10: Who reviews commercial Statements of Work?
* **Answer:** SOWs are drafted by sales and approved by Shubham Jain or Kamlesh Kushwah.

### Q11: What pricing structures do we offer?
* **Answer:** We offer monthly dedicated developer retainers and Time & Materials sprint rates.

### Q12: How do we handle international billing?
* **Answer:** Invoices are generated in USD or EUR and paid via bank transfers.

### Q13: What is the discount policy for startups?
* **Answer:** Startups with seed funding can request up to 15% discount on early sprints.

### Q14: How do we secure project renewals?
* **Answer:** Through quarterly feedback check-ins and performance KPI tracking.

### Q15: Who coordinates marketing content creation?
* **Answer:** Neha Gupta coordinates the marketing team and content writers.

### Q16: What SEO keywords are target metrics?
* **Answer:** Keywords: Flutter developer Indore, IoT development company, SaaS engineering.

### Q17: How is client satisfaction measured?
* **Answer:** CSAT reviews are conducted mid-project and post-launch via forms.

### Q18: Where do we publish case studies?
* **Answer:** Case studies are published on inurum.com/case-studies and in the company repository.

### Q19: What is the process to draft a custom NDA?
* **Answer:** Use the NDA template in the contracts folder and consult legal counsel.

### Q20: How is a client kickoff meeting scheduled?
* **Answer:** Kickoff meetings are scheduled within 48 hours of contract signing.

### Q21: What resources are assigned to sales calls?
* **Answer:** Sales calls are supported by a technical architect (usually Shivansh Kushwah).

### Q22: Do we participate in industrial conferences?
* **Answer:** Yes, we participate in IoT and mobile tech conferences in Germany and India.

### Q23: How do we coordinate with Italy partners?
* **Answer:** Via our Legnano office using the coordinate email contact@techcrafters.it.

### Q24: What is our average client retention rate?
* **Answer:** Our customer retention rate is 92% across all enterprise engagements.

### Q25: Where do we track sales commission metrics?
* **Answer:** Commission logs are stored securely in the Finance ERP module.
