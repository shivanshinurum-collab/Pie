# Frequently Asked Questions: Sales Process & Client Onboarding (Part 2)

## Overview
Curated Q&A list covering Sales Process & Client Onboarding at Inurum Technologies - Part 2.

## Detailed Information
### Q26: What target industries do we focus on?
* **Answer:** Healthcare, logistics, manufacturing, AgTech, and retail sectors.

### Q27: How are developer resume profiles shared?
* **Answer:** Vetted profiles are redacted and shared as PDF resumes via HubSpot.

### Q28: What is the standard project kickoff SLA?
* **Answer:** Engineering teams are onboarded and kicked off within 46 to 72 hours.

### Q29: How do we resolve client onboarding conflicts?
* **Answer:** Conflicts are escalated to Kamlesh Kushwah for swift resolution.

### Q30: Can clients interview dedicated developers?
* **Answer:** Yes, clients can perform technical interviews with proposed engineers.

### Q31: What is the initial response SLA for sales inquiries? (Update 1)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Inbound sales inquiries must be answered within 24 hours. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q32: How do I request a free technical consultation? (Update 2)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Clients can schedule a 30-minute call via inurum.com/start. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q33: Who leads business development campaigns? (Update 3)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Shubham Saratha is the BDE lead coordinating sales strategies. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q34: What is the conversion target for sales leads? (Update 4)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Our sales goal is a conversion rate greater than 25% on qualified leads. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q35: Where are new leads logged? (Update 5)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: All customer leads and communications are logged in the HubSpot CRM. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q36: What is the standard duration of an MVP project? (Update 6)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: A standard mobile MVP ranges from 8 to 14 weeks from project kickoff. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q37: How is a project budget estimated? (Update 7)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Estimates are prepared after a tech plan blueprint phase based on sprint rates. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q38: What is the turnaround time for a custom quote? (Update 8)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Custom quotes and feasibility reviews are delivered in 12–24 hours. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q39: How do we market our technical capability? (Update 9)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Through developer blog posts, case studies, and LinkedIn updates. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q40: Who reviews commercial Statements of Work? (Update 10)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: SOWs are drafted by sales and approved by Shubham Jain or Kamlesh Kushwah. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q41: What pricing structures do we offer? (Update 11)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: We offer monthly dedicated developer retainers and Time & Materials sprint rates. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q42: How do we handle international billing? (Update 12)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Invoices are generated in USD or EUR and paid via bank transfers. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q43: What is the discount policy for startups? (Update 13)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Startups with seed funding can request up to 15% discount on early sprints. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q44: How do we secure project renewals? (Update 14)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Through quarterly feedback check-ins and performance KPI tracking. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q45: Who coordinates marketing content creation? (Update 15)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Neha Gupta coordinates the marketing team and content writers. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q46: What SEO keywords are target metrics? (Update 16)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Keywords: Flutter developer Indore, IoT development company, SaaS engineering. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q47: How is client satisfaction measured? (Update 17)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: CSAT reviews are conducted mid-project and post-launch via forms. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q48: Where do we publish case studies? (Update 18)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Case studies are published on inurum.com/case-studies and in the company repository. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q49: What is the process to draft a custom NDA? (Update 19)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Use the NDA template in the contracts folder and consult legal counsel. Aligned with corporate guidelines.

### Q50: How is a client kickoff meeting scheduled? (Update 20)
* **Answer:** Follow-up: Kickoff meetings are scheduled within 48 hours of contract signing. Aligned with corporate guidelines.
