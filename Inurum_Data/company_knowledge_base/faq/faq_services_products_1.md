# Frequently Asked Questions: Services Offering & Custom Products (Part 1)

## Overview
Curated Q&A list covering Services Offering & Custom Products at Inurum Technologies - Part 1.

## Detailed Information
### Q01: What services does Inurum offer?
* **Answer:** Mobile apps, IoT embedded systems, backend microservices, and dedicated developer teams.

### Q02: What is the ELIoT product category?
* **Answer:** ELIoT is a consumer IoT, smart home, and AgTech plant monitoring system.

### Q03: Does ELIoT require a companion mobile app?
* **Answer:** Yes, the ELIoT app displays live telemetry, alerts, and care plans.

### Q04: What sensors are built into the ELIoT wall?
* **Answer:** Ambient temp, light intensity/spectrum, humidity, soil moisture, and soil pH.

### Q05: How long does the ELIoT battery last?
* **Answer:** The custom hardware runs for up to 6 months on a single Qi wireless charge.

### Q06: What is the SmartStarter telematics solution?
* **Answer:** It is an enterprise plug-and-play semi-truck remote engine controller.

### Q07: Does SmartStarter violate truck warranties?
* **Answer:** No, the PCB module connects to OEM harnesses without wiring cuts.

### Q08: What safety loops are built into SmartStarter?
* **Answer:** Auto-start on low battery/temp, pre-trip parameters check, and auto-shutdown.

### Q09: What is the Fruitrack agricultural application?
* **Answer:** A worker productivity and harvest telemetry tracking system built in Flutter.

### Q10: How does Fruitrack handle offline rural environments?
* **Answer:** It uses a local SQLite queue syncing to AWS cloud when connected.

### Q11: What is the data entry speed target for Fruitrack?
* **Answer:** Supervisors log harvest data in under 10 seconds per worker.

### Q12: What is the Current Detection Sensor System?
* **Answer:** An industrial IoT welder monitor logging AC/DC current pulses.

### Q13: Does the current sensor require wiring modifications?
* **Answer:** No, it clamps around the output cable using non-invasive Hall Effect.

### Q14: What metrics does the welder system calculate per pulse?
* **Answer:** Peak/average current, pulse duration, residual current, energy, thermal load.

### Q15: What is the Mobile Refrigeration Control System?
* **Answer:** A telematics cold-chain tracker logging temperature, GPS, and power status.

### Q16: What cellular module is integrated into the cold-chain system?
* **Answer:** Quectel cellular modules transmitting GPS and telemetry over LTE.

### Q17: Does Inurum provide IoT manufacturing support?
* **Answer:** Yes, we deliver Gerber files, BOM, testing jigs, and provisioning scripts.

### Q18: What is the Smart Parichay digital platform?
* **Answer:** An NFC and QR-enabled digital business card platform syncing 50k+ profiles.

### Q19: What is Creative Construction Ledger (CCL)?
* **Answer:** A multi-tenant cost estimation and management SaaS for construction projects.

### Q20: What is Worthwhiz Financials?
* **Answer:** A premium stock research and investment advisory SaaS platform.

### Q21: Does Inurum build custom CRM software?
* **Answer:** We integrate third-party CRMs and build custom database APIs where needed.

### Q22: What is the standard framework for web admin panels?
* **Answer:** We build React-based dashboards using modern design libraries.

### Q23: How do we secure streaming APIs in eBaba TV?
* **Answer:** Using AWS CloudFront signed cookies and token-based HLS restrictions.

### Q24: What geolocation libraries are used in FamConnect?
* **Answer:** We use custom native background location trackers for iOS and Android.

### Q25: What database is utilized for the school ERP portal?
* **Answer:** DgDiary India uses MySQL databases paired with secure PHP backends.
