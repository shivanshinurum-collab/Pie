# Frequently Asked Questions: Operations, Logistics & Client Support (Part 1)

## Overview
Curated Q&A list covering Operations, Logistics & Client Support at Inurum Technologies - Part 1.

## Detailed Information
### Q01: What is the coordinates location of Indore headquarters?
* **Answer:** Coordinates are 22.7196° N, 75.8577° E PU4 Indore.

### Q02: What timezone does the Legnano office operate in?
* **Answer:** Operations run in Central European Time (CET/CEST) UTC+1.

### Q03: How do I contact the Legnano operations partner?
* **Answer:** Email contact@techcrafters.it or call +39 03311586334.

### Q04: What is the support contact email for active clients?
* **Answer:** Active clients can email support@inurum.com for quick ticket routing.

### Q05: How are client support tickets prioritized?
* **Answer:** By severity level matching SLA parameters in the contracts.

### Q06: Who manages the physical assets in the Indore office?
* **Answer:** Kamlesh Kushwah manages the office facilities and engineering lab.

### Q07: What is the SLA response time for Tier 2 support?
* **Answer:** Tier 2 support guarantees response in under 2 hours for critical issues.

### Q08: Where are physical hardware prototypes tested?
* **Answer:** Tested in our dedicated hardware lab in Indore Scheme No 54.

### Q09: How do we ship hardware prototypes to Italy?
* **Answer:** Shipped via DHL/FedEx with compliance paperwork managed by operations.

### Q10: Who maintains office access permissions?
* **Answer:** IT support manages badge credentials and cloud IAM permissions.

### Q11: What is the average experience level of Inurum engineers?
* **Answer:** The average experience level is 8+ years across all technology stacks.

### Q12: What is the talent retention rate at Inurum?
* **Answer:** We maintain a 96% talent retention rate for engineering positions.

### Q13: How fast can Inurum onboard a new developer?
* **Answer:** Onboarding and workspace access are completed in under 48 hours.

### Q14: What is the average time-to-hire for new recruits?
* **Answer:** The recruitment time-to-hire average is 14 days.

### Q15: Are all developers proficient in English?
* **Answer:** Yes, we guarantee 100% English proficiency across all teams.

* **Answer:** Shubham Jain and Kamlesh Kushwah are the co-owners.

### Q17: Does Inurum have ISO certification?
* **Answer:** Yes, Inurum is an ISO 9001 certified organization for quality management.

### Q18: Are our cloud environments SOC2 certified?
* **Answer:** Our designs follow SOC2 and HIPAA-ready architecture requirements.

### Q19: What is the response target on general project inquiries?
* **Answer:** Under 24 hours response time on all project inquiries.

### Q20: Where do employees report building facilities issues?
* **Answer:** Raise tickets in Slack channel #facilities or contact operations.

### Q21: How do we handle client site visits?
* **Answer:** Coordinated via Shubham Saratha with accommodation support in Indore/Legnano.

### Q22: Are clients given access to Jira workspaces?
* **Answer:** Yes, clients receive observer permissions on their active boards.

### Q23: What backup power exists in the Indore headquarters?
* **Answer:** The facility is backed by a 50kVA diesel generator and online UPS systems.

### Q24: How do we verify visitor identity at the office?
* **Answer:** Visitors must sign in at reception and carry guest badges.

### Q25: Are developer workstations secured physically?
