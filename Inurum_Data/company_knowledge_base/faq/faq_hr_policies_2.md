# Frequently Asked Questions: Human Resources & Company Policies (Part 2)

## Overview
Curated Q&A list covering Human Resources & Company Policies at Inurum Technologies - Part 2.

## Detailed Information
### Q26: What support is provided during bereavement?
* **Answer:** Employees receive 5 days of compassionate leave for immediate family members.

### Q27: How do I update my emergency contact details?
* **Answer:** Update details directly in the profile editor of the ERP portal.

### Q28: Are internships offered at Inurum?
* **Answer:** Yes, we run a 6-month developer internship program starting in January and July.

### Q29: Is moonlighting permitted?
* **Answer:** No, employees are not permitted to engage in external employment or freelance work.

### Q30: What is the company policy on gift acceptance?
* **Answer:** Employees cannot accept client gifts exceeding $50 in value without approval.

### Q31: How are overtime hours compensated?
* **Answer:** Overtime hours must be pre-approved and are compensated or given as comp-off.

### Q32: What is the policy for medical emergencies at the office?
* **Answer:** First-aid kits are available at reception; immediately contact Kamlesh Kushwah.

### Q33: Are training materials provided to freshers?
* **Answer:** Yes, freshers undergo a structured 4-week training plan using our handbooks.

### Q34: Can leaves be combined with WFH?
* **Answer:** Yes, with prior email confirmation from the department head.

### Q35: What is the exit handover document format?
* **Answer:** Handovers must contain codebase walkthroughs, open tickets, and credentials transfer.

### Q36: Are there rewards for developer of the month?
* **Answer:** Yes, recognized developers receive a certificate and a shopping voucher.

### Q37: How do I apply for short term unpaid leave?
* **Answer:** Apply via HR portal and schedule a formal discussion with Priya Mehta.

### Q38: What support is provided to relocate to Indore?
* **Answer:** Relocation assistance covers 15 days of accommodation and travel expenses.

### Q39: Does the company provide transport support for late shifts?
* **Answer:** Cabs are arranged for employees working past 09:00 PM IST.

### Q40: Is there an employee grievance committee?
* **Answer:** Yes, led by Shubham Jain, Kamlesh Kushwah, and Priya Mehta.

### Q41: How is compliance with ISO 9001 ensured?
* **Answer:** Through annual external quality audits and weekly project reviews.

### Q42: What is the policy on social media posts about clients?
* **Answer:** Posting confidential project metrics or client details is strictly prohibited.

### Q43: Can I request an advance on my salary?
* **Answer:** Salary advances are approved only for medical emergencies, up to 1 month salary.

### Q44: What is the employee retention bonus timeline?
* **Answer:** Bonuses are paid at the end of 12 and 24 months of continuous service.

### Q45: Who manages the Indore office operations?
* **Answer:** Kamlesh Kushwah manages all local facility operations and testing jigs.

### Q46: Are training records audited?
* **Answer:** Yes, HR reviews training records quarterly to verify certification updates.

### Q47: What is the travel policy class of travel?
* **Answer:** Economy class for all flights under 6 hours; business class requires CEO approval.

### Q48: How do I claim per diem during travel?
* **Answer:** Log per diem claims matching the travel SOP rate for India/Europe.

### Q49: Are client dinners reimbursable?
* **Answer:** Yes, client entertainment expenses are reimbursable with itemized receipts.

### Q50: What is the exit clearance checklist?
* **Answer:** IT access revocation, physical asset return, final timesheet signoff, and HR exit interview.
